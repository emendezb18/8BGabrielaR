Brief history of the participating countries 15 years after the Kosovo War?

The Kosovo war was fought by the ethnic Albanians who opposed Serbia's rules and the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia and the government ( also like the republics of Serbia and Montenegro). Those people were called the Kosovo Albanian rebels, known as the Kosovo Liberation Army.
